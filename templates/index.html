ABOUT CAMPUSPULSE

Smart College Event Management System

CampusPulse is a modern Smart College Event Management System designed to simplify college event organization and student participation. Students can explore events, register online, make secure payments, track registrations, download certificates, submit feedback, and monitor their event participation from a single dashboard. Administrators can easily manage students, events, attendance, payments, reports, certificates and analytics through one powerful platform.
